Safety Guide

🛡️

Stay Safe While Rescuing

Essential tips for safe animal rescue

01

Approach with Caution

▼

Always approach a stray animal slowly and calmly. Avoid sudden movements or loud noises. Let the animal see and smell you before attempting to touch it.

💡 Crouch down to appear less threatening. Extend your hand slowly for the animal to sniff.
02

Assess the Situation

▼

Check the animal's behavior for signs of injury, illness, or aggression. A wagging tail doesn't always mean friendliness — observe body language carefully.

💡 Look for limping, bleeding, difficulty breathing, or discharge from eyes and nose.
03

Use Protection

▼

Wear gloves or use a cloth when handling injured animals. This protects both you and the animal. If the animal is aggressive, do not attempt to handle it alone.

💡 Keep a basic rescue kit in your car: gloves, towel, cardboard box, and water.
04

Provide First Aid

▼

For minor wounds, clean gently with water. For serious injuries, keep the animal warm and still. Do not give human medication to animals.

💡 A calm, confined space (like a box with a towel) helps reduce shock and stress.
05

Contact Help

▼

Call a local animal rescue organization or veterinarian immediately. Document the location and condition using this app to speed up the rescue process.

💡 Take clear photos and note landmarks near the animal's location.
